Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

WizeLife ("we", "us", "our") operates the website wizelife.ai and the suite of AI tools accessible through it — WizeMoney, WizeTravel, WizeTax, WizeHealth, and WizeDeal. Our contact email is hi@wizelife.ai.

2. What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data: your email address, display name, and password hash (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Usage data: financial transactions, goals, and settings you enter — stored in Firestore under your user ID.
  • Analytics data: page views, session duration, and browser/device type — collected via Google Analytics only if you accept cookies.
  • AI queries: the questions you send to our AI assistant. These are processed by third-party AI APIs and are not stored permanently by us.

3. Why We Collect It

  • To provide and personalise the service (account auth, syncing your data).
  • To enforce fair-use limits (daily AI quota per plan).
  • To understand how users interact with WizeLife so we can improve it (analytics, with your consent).

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than operating the service.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication & Firestore database. Data stored on Google infrastructure (EU/US). Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — Used only with your explicit consent. Collects anonymised usage metrics. Google Privacy Policy.
  • AI APIs — Your queries are sent to AI processing services. Queries are not stored by us beyond your session.

5. Cookies

We use a single first-party cookie to remember your cookie consent choice. Google Analytics cookies are set only after you click "Accept" in the cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email us at hi@wizelife.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

Your account data and Firestore records are retained until you delete your account. Deleting your account removes all associated data from our Firestore database within 30 days.

8. Security

All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Firestore security rules ensure each user can only access their own data. We use Cloudflare for DDoS protection and WAF.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be noted at the top with a new date. Continued use of WizeLife after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hi@wizelife.ai.
